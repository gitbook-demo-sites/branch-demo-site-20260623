# Account setup and maintenance

Find solutions for signing up, logging in, activation links, account closure, notifications, address, app, email, and phone number updates.

## Account creation and sign up

Use Branch mobile app onboarding for most flows. Desktop sign-up may be limited depending on employer or partner setup.

## Login issues

Reset your passcode, verify your phone number, and use the support chat when text codes do not arrive.

{% hint style="info" icon="life-ring" %}
Still need help? Use Branch app chat when you can access the app. If you cannot log in, use Help Center chat and include the relevant account, card, payout, or transaction context.
{% endhint %}
