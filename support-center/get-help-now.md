# Get Help Now

Need help? Start with Branch app chat from the account tab, contact Branch Support when you cannot access the app, and follow security best practices for protecting your account.

## Need help?

Get fast help through chat in your app account tab. If you cannot log in, use Help Center chat and include the account details requested by support.

## Security Best Practices

Never share one-time codes, protect your passcode, verify messages before clicking links, and contact support if you suspect unauthorized account activity.
