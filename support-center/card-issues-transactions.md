# Card issues and transactions

Information about ordering and activating cards, pending or declined transactions, card controls, rewards, account statements, fraud, refunds, and recurring payments.

## Card controls

Use the Wallet tab and card controls to activate, lock, unlock, order replacements, reset PIN, and add the card to Apple Pay or Google Wallet.

## Transactions

Review pending transactions, declined purchases, merchant refunds, unauthorized charges, and account statements before filing a dispute.

Still need help? Use Branch app chat when you can access the app. If you cannot log in, use Help Center chat and include the relevant account, card, payout, or transaction context.
